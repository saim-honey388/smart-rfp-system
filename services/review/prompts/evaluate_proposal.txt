Goal: score each proposal against one RFP, based ONLY on the given context.

- experience: ONE SENTENCE summary of past experience mentioned.
- methodology: ONE SENTENCE summary of the technical approach/methodology.
- warranties: ONE SENTENCE summary of warranties/SLA mentioned.
- timeline_details: ONE SENTENCE summary of the timeline breakdown.

Respond with STRICT JSON ONLY, matching this shape:
